About

A little about my background

I joined Kabete National Polytechnic in August 2023, where I pursued a Higher Diploma (Level 6) in ICT. Since then I've built more than a year of hands-on experience across networking, hardware repair, systems administration, helpdesk support, web design and graphic design.

While I'm comfortable across the full ICT stack, I specialize in networking, web design, graphic design and helpdesk support — from wiring and configuring LANs/WANs to designing clean, functional websites and visuals, to being the calm first point of contact when something breaks.

I enjoy solving problems that keep people and offices connected — whether that's tracing a faulty cable, resetting a locked-out machine, or setting up a network from scratch.
